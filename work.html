What Clients Usually Need

Every business is different. These options reflect what most clients usually need. We'll always tailor the final setup to your project.

Basic

Best for getting started

A straightforward website that gets your business online quickly with essential pages and clear contact options.

  • 3-5 pages
  • Contact form
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Basic SEO setup

Standard

Most common choice

A more complete website with additional pages and features that help you showcase your business and attract customers.

  • 5-8 pages
  • Enhanced design
  • Additional features
  • Comprehensive SEO

Pro

For growing or established businesses

A fully customized website built around your specific needs, with advanced features and integrations.

  • Unlimited pages
  • Custom features
  • Third-party integrations
  • Advanced SEO & analytics
